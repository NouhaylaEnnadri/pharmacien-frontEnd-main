Certifications


SERVICE BIOLOGIQUE SARL has obtained ISO 13485:2016 certification for its activities related to the import, export, storage, and distribution of medical devices and in-vitro diagnostic reagents. ISO 13485 is the most rigorous international standard in the world, and SERVICE BIOLOGIQUE was audited by the European body CERTI-TRUST. This certification rewards and strengthens the quality approach that SERVICE BIOLOGIQUE> has chosen to support its growth, ensuring the quality and safety of products by demonstrating the rigorous quality procedures required to provide safe and effective medical devices. However, obtaining ISO recognition is not an endpoint; we are constantly seeking to improve our Quality Management System processes to meet the expectations and requirements of our clients.
